### Blepharoplasty Overview

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery) is designed to rejuvenate the eyes by addressing:

- Excess upper eyelid skin ( _“hooding”_)
- Under-eye bags and puffiness
- Fine wrinkles and skin laxity
- A tired or aged appearance

👉 The goal is a **refreshed, natural look—not a “surgical” appearance**

## Consult with Our Utah Eyelid Surgeon

- **Subtle, natural enhancement**
- Preservation of a **youthful eyelid shape**
- Avoidance of a hollowed or over-tightened look
- Careful attention to both **function and aesthetics**

## WHAT AGING DOES TO THE EYELIDS

![Eyelid Surgery Utah](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/dwdwd.png)

![Upper and Lower Blepharoplasty](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/dsdsds.png)

**Changes include:**

- **Skin laxity** → upper eyelid hooding
- **Fat protrusion** → under-eye “bags”
- **Volume loss** → hollowing (tear trough)
- **Muscle laxity** → lower lid sagging

👉 Effective treatment requires a **balanced approach—not just skin removal**

![](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/fsfsfsf.png)

## UPPER EYELID BLEPHAROPLASTY

![Eyelid Surgery](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/fgffg.jpg)

**What It Treats**

- Excess skin (hooding)
- Heaviness of the upper eyelids
- Visual obstruction (in some cases)

**Procedure Highlights**

- Incision placed in the **natural eyelid crease**
- Removal of excess skin ± small amount of fat
- Scar typically **well-hidden and nearly invisible**

👉 Creates a more **open, refreshed eye appearance**

![Upper and Lower Eyelid Incisions](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/gsssssss.jpg)

![open, refreshed eye appearance](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/ghhghgh.jpg)

## LOWER EYELID BLEPHAROPLASTY

![Lower Eyelid Blepharoplasty](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/nhnhnhnhnh.png)

**What It Treats**

- Under-eye bags
- Puffiness
- Loose or excess skin
- Tear trough hollowing (in select cases)

**Techniques**

- **Transconjunctival (no external scar)**
  - Fat repositioning/removal through inner eyelid
- **Transcutaneous (skin + muscle tightening)**
  - Small incision just below lashes

👉 Modern approaches emphasize **fat repositioning—not aggressive removal** to avoid hollowing

![](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/vhhhhhhhh.png)

## THE IMPORTANCE OF VOLUME & BALANCE

A youthful eye is defined by:

- A **smooth transition** from eyelid to cheek
- Minimal hollowing
- Proper support of the lower eyelid

👉 In some patients, **fat repositioning or grafting** is key to natural results

### CANDIDACY

**You may be a good candidate if you:**

- Have upper eyelid heaviness or hooding
- Notice under-eye bags or puffiness
- Feel your eyes appear tired despite adequate rest

Are in good overall health

### RECOVERY

**First 5–7 Days**

- Swelling and bruising
- Use of cold compresses
- Light activity

**7–10 Days**

- Sutures removed (if present)
- Return to social activities

**2–3 Weeks**

- Most swelling resolves

**4–6 Weeks**

- Final refinement

### Blepharoplasty Risks and Safety

![](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/fcfcfc.png)

![](https://utah-aesthetic-surgery.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/bgggggg.jpg)

Risks may include:

- Temporary swelling or bruising
- Dry eyes or irritation
- Asymmetry
- Lower eyelid malposition (rare with proper technique)
- Scarring (typically minimal)

👉 Careful technique and conservative planning minimize these risks

#### Blepharoplasty Results

**Patients can expect:**

- More open, refreshed eyes
- Reduction in under-eye bags
- Improved upper eyelid contour
- A more rested, youthful appearance

👉 Results appear **natural—not “operated on”**

### LONG-TERM OUTCOME

- Long-lasting results (often 10+ years for upper eyelids)
- Aging continues naturally
- Patients maintain a more youthful baseline

### COMMON QUESTIONS

Will I look different?

You will look like a **more rested version of yourself**, not different.

Are scars visible?

Upper eyelid scars are hidden in the crease; lower scars are minimal or internal.

Is this painful?

Discomfort is typically mild and well-controlled.

Can this be combined with other procedures?

Yes—commonly combined with facelift or fat grafting for full facial rejuvenation.
