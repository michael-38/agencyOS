#### Clinical Experience & Focus

**With over two decades of surgical experience, Dr. Rodrigues has developed expertise across a full spectrum of aesthetic procedures, including:**

- Breast augmentation and breast rejuvenation
- Abdominoplasty and body contouring
- Fat transfer and gluteal enhancement
- Facial aesthetic surgery
- Comprehensive cosmetic surgery of the face, breast, and body

**His approach emphasizes:**

- **Natural, balanced results**
- **Meticulous surgical technique**
- **Thoughtful, individualized care**

#### Philosophy of Care

**Dr. Rodrigues believes that aesthetic surgery is both a science and an art. His practice is centered on:**

- Achieving harmony and proportion—not exaggeration
- Prioritizing patient safety and clear communication
- Recommending the most appropriate treatment—even when non-surgical

Each patient receives a **customized surgical plan** tailored to their anatomy, goals, and long-term outcomes.

#### Commitment to Excellence

Dr. Rodrigues has completed extensive postgraduate training and ongoing professional development focused on:

- Surgical safety and best practices
- Advanced aesthetic techniques
- Patient communication and outcomes optimization

He remains committed to maintaining the highest standards of care through continuous education and refinement of technique.
