Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation at Utah Aesthetic Surgery enhances volume and restores balance with silicone or saline implants — or your own fat. Dr. Rodrigues tailors implant type, profile, and placement (submuscular, subglandular, or subfascial) to your anatomy for a natural upper-pole slope, never an overfilled look. Incisions stay discreetly hidden in the breast crease, areola, or armpit.

02 / 06UAS · Plastic Surgery

Brazilian Butt Lift

A Brazilian Butt Lift (buttock fat transfer) sculpts the waist and adds natural projection using your own purified fat — no implants. Dr. Rodrigues places fat only in the safe subcutaneous layer, never intramuscular, following modern BBL safety protocols to build a smooth S-curve and defined waist-to-hip ratio. Liposuction of the abdomen, flanks, and lower back is included; 60–80% of transferred fat survives long-term.

Surgery2.5–4 hrs
AnesthesiaGeneral
Recovery2 wks no sitting · 6 wks
Startingfrom $8,500

Tummy Tuck

A tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) removes loose abdominal skin and repairs separated core muscles after pregnancy or weight loss — for a flatter, firmer midsection. Dr. Rodrigues uses a thoughtful low "bikini" scar and meticulous technique with DVT-prevention protocols. Often combined with liposuction or a BBL for a full mommy-makeover body-contouring plan.

Surgery2–4 hrs
AnesthesiaGeneral
Recovery2 wks · 6 wks full
Startingfrom $7,500

Facelift & Neck Lift

Face & neck rejuvenation (facelift) restores a refreshed, age-appropriate look by repositioning the deeper SMAS layer — not just pulling skin. Dr. Rodrigues softens jowls, sharpens the jawline, and smooths neck banding through discreet incisions hidden around the ear and hairline. Frequently paired with eyelid surgery or fat grafting; results typically last 10+ years.

Surgery3–5 hrs
AnesthesiaGeneral
Recovery2–3 wks social

05 / 06UAS · Plastic Surgery

Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty (nose reshaping) refines the bridge, tip, and proportion of the nose while keeping it in harmony with the rest of your face. Drawing on a sculptor's eye and structural grafting, Dr. Rodrigues performs cosmetic, functional (breathing), and revision rhinoplasty — open or closed. The goal is a natural result, noticed subtly and never "overdone."

Surgery2–3 hrs
AnesthesiaGeneral
Recovery1–2 wks splint
Startingfrom $7,500
06 / 06UAS · Plastic Surgery

Eyelid Surgery

Eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty) lifts heavy, hooded upper lids and smooths under-eye bags for a rested, more open look. Dr. Rodrigues favors fat repositioning over aggressive removal to avoid a hollowed appearance; upper-lid scars hide in the natural crease and lower-lid work is often scarless (transconjunctival). Upper-eyelid results often last 10+ years.

Surgery1–2 hrs
AnesthesiaLocal/IV or general
Recovery7–10 days
Startingfrom $3,800

Where is Utah Aesthetic Surgery located, and which areas do you serve?
Utah Aesthetic Surgery is located at 10382 S. Jordan Gateway, Suite 100, South Jordan, UT 84095, just off I-15 in the South Jordan Gateway area. We serve patients throughout Salt Lake County — including Draper, Riverton, Sandy, West Jordan, Murray, and the greater Salt Lake City area. Free parking is available in the building lot, and Suite 100 is on the ground floor.
How much does a Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL) cost in South Jordan, UT?
A Brazilian Butt Lift at Utah Aesthetic Surgery starts at $8,500–$14,000, depending on the volume of liposuction required. Your quote includes the surgeon's fee, anesthesia, the included liposuction, and accredited-facility costs. You'll receive an exact, itemized quote in writing at your complimentary consultation.
How much does breast augmentation or a tummy tuck cost?
Breast augmentation starts at $6,500–$10,000 (including implants, anesthesia, and facility fees), and a tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) starts at $7,500. Final pricing depends on implant type (silicone vs. saline), technique, and surgical complexity. Every quoted price includes surgeon, anesthesia, and facility fees — no hidden costs.
Do you offer financing or payment plans?
Yes — Utah Aesthetic Surgery works with CareCredit and Cherry to offer flexible monthly payment plans, alongside all major credit cards. Many patients finance their procedure with low- or no-interest plans for approved credit. Ask about financing during your free consultation or call (801) 810-0761.
Is Dr. Rodrigues a board-certified plastic surgeon?
Yes. Dr. Robert Rodrigues is a double board-certified plastic surgeon — certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Surgery. He completed fellowship and residency training at the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center, Case Western Reserve University, the University of Michigan, and the Mayo Clinic, and has cared for Utah patients since 2008. Every procedure is performed personally by Dr. Rodrigues.
Will my surgery hurt, and what type of anesthesia is used?
Most body and facial procedures — BBL, breast augmentation, tummy tuck, facelift, and rhinoplasty — are performed under general anesthesia in an accredited surgical facility; eyelid surgery may use local anesthesia with IV sedation. Postoperative discomfort is managed with a prescribed pain protocol — most patients describe soreness rather than sharp pain for the first 3–5 days.
How long does surgery take, and when can I return to work?
Operative times range from about 1–2 hours (breast augmentation, eyelid surgery) to 3–5 hours (facelift). Most patients return to desk work in 10–14 days for body procedures and 2–3 weeks after a facelift. Full activity and exercise typically resume at 6 weeks. Detailed timelines are listed on each procedure above.
Is a Brazilian Butt Lift safe?
Buttock fat transfer is safe when performed with strict modern technique. Dr. Rodrigues places fat only in the safe subcutaneous layer — never intramuscular — with careful cannula control, in line with current plastic-surgery safety recommendations. All surgery carries some risk; these are reviewed fully and honestly at your consultation.
Will my results look natural, or "overdone"?
Natural, balanced results are the entire philosophy of the practice. Dr. Rodrigues sculpts to your individual anatomy — proportion over exaggeration — so you look like a refreshed version of yourself, not "operated on." If a request risks an artificial result, he will tell you honestly.
What if I'm not a good candidate, or I'm unhappy with my result?
Dr. Rodrigues gives honest guidance. If surgery isn't right for you, or a non-surgical option is better, he'll say so — he has discouraged procedures that weren't in a patient's interest. Realistic expectations are set during consultation, and revision options are always discussed openly.
How much does a consultation cost?
Consultations at Utah Aesthetic Surgery are complimentary and no-obligation. You'll meet Dr. Rodrigues personally, receive a customized surgical plan and a written price quote, and have every question answered — with no pressure to book. Call (801) 810-0761 or request a time below.
Are breast implants permanent, and can procedures be combined?
Breast implants are not lifetime devices — periodic imaging is recommended for silicone implants, and replacement may be needed over time. Many patients combine procedures (for example, a tummy tuck with breast augmentation as a "mommy makeover," or a facelift with eyelid surgery) in a single surgery to shorten overall recovery. Candidacy is reviewed at your consultation.
