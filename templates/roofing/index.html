Common questions

Quick answers so you know what to expect before you call or book.

Is the roof inspection really free? ▾
Yes. We inspect your roof and explain what we find at no charge and with no obligation to hire us. If you move forward, you’ll get a written estimate first.
Do you work with insurance on storm damage? ▾
We document damage with photos, meet adjusters when needed, and help you understand your policy so you can make informed decisions—without unnecessary upsells.
How soon can you come out? ▾
Most inspections are scheduled within 48 hours. After major storms we prioritize leaks and safety issues and communicate realistic timing when you call.
What warranties do you offer? ▾
Manufacturer warranties apply to materials; we provide a written workmanship warranty on our labor so you’re covered on both.
Which areas do you serve? ▾
Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Georgetown, Pflugerville, Leander, Bee Cave, Lakeway, and nearby communities. Not sure? Call with your address and we’ll confirm.
