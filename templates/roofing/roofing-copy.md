# Roofing Contractor — Landing Page Template Structure

> **Primary goal:** Convert visitors into calls, form submissions, or booked consultations.
> **Visitor psychology:** Most roofing visitors are in one of two modes — **emergency** (storm damage, active leak) or **planned** (aging roof, shopping for replacement). The page must serve both instantly.

---

## Global Conversion Elements (Persistent on Every Scroll Position)

### Sticky Header
- **Logo** (left)
- **Phone number** (large, bold, click-to-call on mobile) — e.g., `(555) 123-4567`
- **"Free Inspection" CTA button** (right, contrasting color) → scrolls to booking form or opens calendar widget
- Hamburger menu (mobile) with anchor links to page sections
- **Hours badge** — "Available 24/7 for Emergencies" or "Mon–Sat 7am–7pm"

### Floating Mobile CTA Bar (Mobile Only)
- Fixed to bottom of screen
- Three buttons side by side: **Call** | **Text Us** | **Book Online**
- Always visible, never dismissed

### Floating Chat/Text Widget (Bottom Right, Desktop)
- "Need help now? Text us" → opens SMS or chat
- Minimal, doesn't block content

---

## Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)

**Purpose:** Answer "Am I in the right place?" in under 3 seconds. Give them one action to take.

### Layout
- **Full-width background:** High-quality photo of the company's crew on a completed roof, or a dramatic before/after. NOT a stock photo.
- **Dark overlay** (40-60% opacity) for text readability

### Content
- **Headline (H1):**
  `[City]'s Most Trusted Roofing Contractor`
  or for storm markets:
  `Storm Damage? Get Your Free Roof Inspection Today`

- **Subheadline (H2):**
  `[X]+ Years Protecting [City] Homes • Licensed & Insured • 5-Star Rated`

- **Trust badges row** (immediately below subheadline):
  - Google rating (stars + review count)
  - "Licensed & Insured"
  - GAF/Owens Corning Certified (if applicable)
  - BBB Accredited
  - "Free Estimates"

- **Primary CTA button:**
  `Get My Free Inspection →`
  Large, high-contrast color (orange, red, or green depending on brand)

- **Secondary CTA (text link below button):**
  `Or call us now: (555) 123-4567`

### Mobile Optimization
- Headline and CTA must be fully visible without scrolling
- Phone number must be tap-to-call
- Background image compressed for speed (<200KB)

## Section 2: Emergency Banner (Conditional/Optional)

**Purpose:** Catch emergency visitors who need help RIGHT NOW.

### Layout
- Full-width colored banner (yellow/orange alert style)
- Only displayed during storm season or active weather events

### Content
- **Icon:** ⚠️ or storm icon
- **Text:** `Storm damage in [City]? We're dispatching crews now. Call for priority service.`
- **CTA:** `Call Now: (555) 123-4567` (click-to-call)

## Section 3: Services Overview

**Purpose:** Confirm they offer what the visitor needs. Create mental anchors for different service types.

### Layout
- 3 or 4 cards in a grid (2x2 on mobile)
- Each card is clickable (anchors to more detail or a dedicated page)

### Cards
1. **Roof Replacement**
   - Icon: house with new roof
   - 1-line description: "Complete tear-off and installation with manufacturer-backed warranties"
   - "Learn More →"

2. **Storm Damage Repair**
   - Icon: lightning/storm
   - 1-line description: "Emergency repairs and full insurance claim assistance"
   - "Get Emergency Help →" (links to call or form)

3. **Roof Inspection**
   - Icon: magnifying glass on roof
   - 1-line description: "Free comprehensive inspection with detailed photo report"
   - "Schedule Inspection →"

4. **Roof Repair**
   - Icon: wrench/tools
   - 1-line description: "Leaks, missing shingles, flashing, and gutter repair"
   - "Request Repair →"

## Section 5: Social Proof — Reviews

**Purpose:** Third-party validation. This is the #1 trust builder for home services.

### Layout
- Section headline: `What Your Neighbors Are Saying`
- 3 review cards in a horizontal scroll/carousel
- Each card shows: **Star rating**, **Review text** (2-3 sentences max), **Reviewer first name + city**, **"Posted on Google"** badge
- **Below cards:** `See All [X] Reviews on Google →` (links to their Google Business Profile)

### Selection Criteria for Displayed Reviews
- Prioritize reviews that mention: specific service (replacement, storm repair), price fairness, speed, professionalism, insurance help
- Must be real Google reviews, not fabricated

---

## Section 6: Conversion Block #1 — Booking Form

**Purpose:** Primary conversion point for visitors who've scrolled this far. They're interested — make it effortless.

### Layout
- Full-width section with contrasting background color
- Two-column on desktop: left = copy, right = form
- Single column on mobile: copy above, form below

### Left Column — Copy
- **Headline:** `Get Your Free Roof Inspection`
- **Subtext:** `We'll send a certified inspector to evaluate your roof — no cost, no obligation. Most inspections completed within 48 hours.`
- **What to expect list:**
  - ✓ Thorough inspection with drone and on-roof photos
  - ✓ Detailed written report of findings
  - ✓ Honest recommendation — repair or replace
  - ✓ Insurance claim guidance if storm damage is found

### Right Column — Form
- **Fields (keep minimal — every field reduces conversion):**
  1. Full Name (required)
  2. Phone Number (required)
  3. Email (required)
  4. Address or Zip Code (required — they need to know the service area)
  5. How can we help? (dropdown: Roof Replacement / Storm Damage / Leak Repair / Inspection / Other)
  6. Preferred contact method (radio: Call Me / Text Me / Email)

- **Submit button:** `Schedule My Free Inspection →`
- **Below button:** `Or call us directly: (555) 123-4567`
- **Privacy line:** `We respect your privacy. No spam, ever.`

### Alternative: Calendar Booking Widget
- Embed Calendly/Cal.com/GoHighLevel calendar widget
- Visitor picks a date/time for inspection or callback
- **Label:** `Pick a Time That Works for You`
- Auto-sends confirmation text + email

---

## Section 7: Before & After Gallery

**Purpose:** Visual proof of quality work. Roofers have a huge advantage here — the transformation is dramatic.

### Layout
- Section headline: `Our Recent Work in [City]`
- 3-4 before/after image sliders (drag to compare)
- Each project labeled: neighborhood/city + service type (e.g., "Full Replacement — Oakwood Subdivision, [City]")
- `See More Projects →` link (if portfolio page exists)

### Notes
- Use REAL project photos, never stock
- Drone shots are incredibly effective for roofing — show the full roof
- Compress images aggressively for page speed

---

## Section 8: How It Works — Process

**Purpose:** Reduce anxiety about "what happens if I call?" Make the next step feel safe and known.

### Layout
- Horizontal timeline or numbered steps (3-4 steps)

### Steps
1. **Call or Book Online** — "Reach out and we'll schedule your free inspection within 48 hours"
2. **Free Inspection & Report** — "Our certified inspector evaluates your roof and provides a detailed photo report"
3. **Clear Estimate** — "You receive an honest, written estimate. No hidden fees, no pressure"
4. **Expert Installation** — "Our crew completes the work, cleans up, and we do a final walkthrough with you"

---

## Section 9: Service Area Map

**Purpose:** Confirm you serve their area. Local relevance = trust.

### Layout
- Embedded Google Map with service area highlighted
- List of cities/neighborhoods served next to the map
- **Headline:** `Proudly Serving [City] and Surrounding Areas`

## Section 10: Conversion Block #2 — Final CTA

**Purpose:** Catch visitors who scrolled the entire page. They're highly engaged — give them one more push.

### Layout
- Full-width, bold background (brand color or dark overlay on a photo)
- Centered text

### Content
- **Headline:** `Ready to Protect Your Home?`
- **Subheadline:** `Free inspections. Honest estimates. No pressure. Ever.`
- **Two CTA buttons side by side:**
  - `Call (555) 123-4567` (click-to-call)
  - `Book My Free Inspection` (scrolls to form or opens calendar)

---

## Section 11: Footer

### Content
- Company name, address, phone, email

- Links: Services, About, Reviews, Contact, Privacy Policy

## SEO Foundations (Built Into Template)

- **Title tag:** `[City] Roofing Contractor | Free Inspections | [Company Name]`
- **Meta description:** `Trusted [City] roofing contractor. Free roof inspections, storm damage repair, and full replacements. Licensed, insured, 5-star rated. Call (555) 123-4567.`
- **H1:** Single, keyword-rich, matches visitor intent
- **Schema markup:** LocalBusiness, AggregateRating, Service
- **NAP consistency:** Name, Address, Phone match Google Business Profile exactly
- **Image alt tags:** Descriptive, include city + service (e.g., "roof replacement in [city] by [company name]")

## Automation Triggers From This Page

Every conversion point feeds into the automation system:

| Trigger | Automation |
|---|---|
| Form submitted | Instant text confirmation + email to client's phone + CRM entry |
| Calendar booked | Confirmation text + email + reminder 24hrs before + reminder 1hr before |
| Missed call | Instant text-back: "Sorry we missed your call! How can we help?" |
| Form submitted but no response in 5 min | Alert to business owner: "HOT LEAD — call now" |
| Estimate sent (manual entry) | Auto follow-up sequence at day 2, 5, 10 |
| Job completed (manual entry) | Review request text at day 1, reminder at day 3 |
