# Plumbing Contractor — Landing Page Template Structure

> **Primary goal:** Convert visitors into calls, form submissions, or booked consultations.
> **Visitor psychology:** Plumbing visitors are overwhelmingly in **emergency mode**. A burst pipe, backed-up sewer, overflowing toilet, or no hot water. They are stressed, in a hurry, and will call the FIRST plumber that looks legitimate. Speed and trust dominate this page — more than any other trade. Secondary visitors are shopping for planned work (water heater replacement, repiping, bathroom rough-in), but emergencies drive the majority of search traffic.

---

## Global Conversion Elements (Persistent on Every Scroll Position)

### Sticky Header
- **Logo** (left)
- **Phone number** (large, bold, click-to-call) — e.g., `(555) 123-4567`
- **"Book a Plumber" CTA button** (right, high contrast)
- **Emergency badge:** `24/7 Emergency Plumbing` — red/orange, always visible
- Hamburger menu (mobile)

### Floating Mobile CTA Bar (Mobile Only)
- Fixed bottom of screen, impossible to miss
- Three buttons: **Call Now** | **Text Us** | **Book Online**
- CRITICAL: Plumbing has the highest emergency rate of any trade. The call button must be enormous and obvious on mobile.

### Floating Chat/Text Widget (Bottom Right, Desktop)
- "Plumbing emergency? Text us now"
- Opens SMS or chat

## Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)

**Purpose:** A plumbing visitor in emergency mode will make a call decision in 5 seconds or less. The hero must instantly communicate: we're available, we're fast, we're trustworthy. No cleverness — just clarity.

### Layout
- Full-width background: Photo of a uniformed, smiling plumber in a clean truck or at a home. Professionalism is the message.
- Dark overlay for text readability

### Content
- **Headline (H1):**
  `[City]'s Trusted Plumber — Fast, Reliable, Fair Prices`
  or emergency-first:
  `Plumbing Emergency? We're On Our Way.`

- **Subheadline (H2):**
  `Available 24/7 • Licensed & Insured • Upfront Pricing — No Surprises`

- **Trust badges row:**
  - Google rating (stars + count)
  - "Licensed & Insured"
  - "24/7 Emergency Service"
  - "No Trip Charge" or "Free Estimates" (if applicable)
  - "Upfront Pricing"
  - BBB badge

- **Primary CTA:**
  `Call Now: (555) 123-4567`
  NOTE: For plumbing, the phone call IS the primary conversion. The CTA should be the phone number itself, styled as a massive click-to-call button. Most plumbing emergencies convert via phone, not forms.

- **Secondary CTA:**
  `Or Book Online →` (scrolls to form/calendar)

### Mobile — Extra Important for Plumbing
- Phone number must be the single most prominent element on screen
- One-tap calling, no scrolling required
- Page must load in under 2 seconds — emergency visitors have zero patience

### Content

## Section 3: Common Problems We Fix

**Purpose:** Different from a "services" section. Plumbing visitors search by PROBLEM, not by service category. They Google "water heater not working" not "water heater installation services." Mirror their language.

### Layout
- 6-8 cards in a grid (2 columns mobile, 3-4 columns desktop)
- Each card uses the customer's problem language, not trade jargon

### Cards

1. **Leaking or Burst Pipes**
   - Icon: water drop / broken pipe
   - "Fast emergency repair to stop water damage"
   - `Get Help Now →`

2. **Clogged Drains & Sewer Backup**
   - Icon: drain / warning
   - "Kitchen, bathroom, and main sewer line clearing"
   - `Fix My Drain →`

3. **Water Heater Problems**
   - Icon: water heater
   - "No hot water? Repair and same-day replacement available"
   - `Restore Hot Water →`

4. **Toilet Repair & Installation**
   - Icon: toilet
   - "Running, leaking, clogged, or needs replacement"
   - `Fix My Toilet →`

5. **Faucet & Fixture Repair**
   - Icon: faucet with drip
   - "Dripping faucets, showerheads, and fixture installation"
   - `Stop the Drip →`

6. **Sewer Line & Repiping**
   - Icon: underground pipe
   - "Camera inspection, trenchless repair, and whole-home repiping"
   - `Schedule Inspection →`

7. **Gas Line Services**
   - Icon: gas flame
   - "Gas leak detection, line installation, and repair"
   - `Get Emergency Help →`

8. **Sump Pump & Water Filtration**
   - Icon: pump / filter
   - "Sump pump repair, installation, and water treatment systems"
   - `Learn More →`

### Why Problem-Based Language Matters
- "Clogged drain" gets 10x the search volume of "drain cleaning service"
- Matching the visitor's words reduces cognitive friction — they feel understood
- Each card can be a dedicated landing page later for SEO/PPC campaigns

- 4-6 columns with icons

## Section 5: Social Proof — Reviews

**Purpose:** Trust is the #1 conversion factor for plumbing. Homeowners are letting a stranger into their home around their plumbing — the most intimate system in the house. Reviews overcome this.

### Layout
- Headline: `See Why [City] Homeowners Call Us First`
- 3 review cards, carousel on mobile
- Stars, quote, name, city, "Google Review" badge
- `Read All [X] Reviews →` (Google Business Profile link)

### Review Selection Criteria
- Prioritize reviews mentioning: fast arrival, honest diagnosis, fair price, clean work, friendly tech
- Gold standard: emergency reviews ("pipe burst at midnight, they were here in 30 minutes, saved our floors")
- Reviews that mention the tech by name add powerful personal trust

## Section 6: Conversion Block #1 — Booking Form

**Purpose:** For non-emergency visitors (water heater quote, repiping estimate, new construction) who prefer to book online rather than call.

### Layout
- Full-width contrasting background
- Two columns desktop: copy left, form right
- Single column mobile

### Left Column — Copy
- **Headline:** `Schedule Your Plumbing Service`
- **Subtext:** `Book in 60 seconds. Same-day appointments usually available.`
- **What to expect:**
  - ✓ Licensed plumber arrives in a marked vehicle, on time
  - ✓ Problem diagnosed and explained before any work starts
  - ✓ Upfront pricing — you approve the cost, no surprises
  - ✓ Work area left clean when we're done

- **Emergency callout box:**
  `⚡ Have an emergency? Don't wait — call us directly: (555) 123-4567`

### Right Column — Form
- Fields (minimal for speed):
  1. Full Name (required)
  2. Phone Number (required)
  3. Email (optional — phone is king in plumbing)
  4. Address or Zip Code (required)
  5. What's the problem? (dropdown: Leaking Pipe / Clogged Drain / Water Heater / Toilet Issue / Sewer Problem / Faucet/Fixture / Other)
  6. Is this an emergency? (toggle — if yes: "Call (555) 123-4567 for immediate help" + still submits form for backup)
  7. Preferred time (dropdown: ASAP / Morning / Afternoon / Specific Date → opens calendar)

- **Submit:** `Send My Request →`
- **Below:** `We respond within 15 minutes during business hours.`
- Privacy line

### Calendar Widget (Below Form or Toggled)
- "Or pick a specific appointment time →"
- Calendly/Cal.com/GHL embed
- Best for planned work (water heater quotes, repiping estimates)

## Section 7: Upfront Pricing Transparency (Optional but Powerful)

**Purpose:** Plumbing has the worst pricing reputation of any home service. Showing any pricing at all — even ranges — massively increases trust and pre-qualifies leads.

### Layout
- Section headline: `Fair, Transparent Pricing`
- Simple table or cards with price ranges

### Content

| Service | Starting At |
|---|---|
| Drain Cleaning | $XX |
| Water Heater Repair | $XX |
| Water Heater Replacement | $XXX |
| Faucet Installation | $XX |
| Toilet Repair | $XX |
| Sewer Camera Inspection | $XX |
| Emergency Service Call | $XX |

- **Disclaimer:** "Final price depends on diagnosis. You always approve the price before work begins."
- **CTA:** `Get Your Exact Price — Call (555) 123-4567`

### Why This Works
- Most competitors hide all pricing → the plumber who shows it looks more honest
- Pre-qualifies callers — fewer "how much?" calls that go nowhere
- Doesn't have to be exact — ranges are fine

## Page Performance Requirements

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Mobile PageSpeed | 95+ (plumbing visitors have the LEAST patience — many are panicking) |
| Largest Contentful Paint | < 2.0s (tighter than other trades) |
| Total page weight | < 1MB (strip everything non-essential) |
| Form load time | Instant |
| Click-to-call | Every phone number, every device |
| SSL | Required |

### Why Speed Is Even More Critical for Plumbing
- Emergency searchers bounce in 3 seconds if the page doesn't load
- They're often searching from a phone while standing in water
- Every second of load time costs conversions at a higher rate than any other trade

## Key Differences From Roofing/HVAC Templates

| Element | Plumbing | Roofing | HVAC |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary CTA** | Phone call (overwhelmingly) | Form/calendar | Mix of call and form |
| **Emergency urgency** | Extreme — every day, year-round | Seasonal (storms) | Seasonal (summer/winter) |
| **Page speed target** | Tightest (95+ / <2s LCP) | Standard (90+ / <2.5s) | Standard (90+ / <2.5s) |
| **Service framing** | Problem-based language | Service-category based | Service-category + seasonal |
| **Pricing transparency** | Highly recommended (trust gap) | Not expected | Not expected |
| **Visitor patience** | Lowest | Moderate | Moderate |
| **Form fields** | Minimal (phone > email) | Standard | Standard |
| **Automation urgency** | 3-min escalation window | 5-min window | 5-min window |
| **Gallery/portfolio** | Not important | Very important (before/after) | Not important |
| **Seasonal swaps** | Not needed | Storm season | Summer/winter |
| **Recurring revenue section** | Not prominent | Not prominent | Maintenance plans = key section |
