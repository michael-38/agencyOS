Section 01 · The diagnosis

Four things costing you patients.

Deficiency 01 · Speed

Your site is too slow.

53%
of mobile site visits are abandoned if a page takes longer than three seconds to load. Google / SOASTA Research, 2017
That's more than half of your ad spend, your Google clicks, and your Instagram traffic — gone before they ever see your work. The patient never gets the chance to decide whether to book.
Deficiency 02 · Discoverability

Patients can't find you.

Your site content isn't optimized for how people actually search — on Google, and increasingly on AI.

Google search

You don't rank for what patients type.

Without optimized service pages, location targeting, and treatment-specific content, you're invisible on the searches that bring high-intent patients.

botox near me med spa [your city] lip filler cost laser resurfacing reviews
AI recommendations

ChatGPT doesn't name you.

When patients ask ChatGPT, Gemini, or Perplexity for the best med spa in their area, those engines pull from structured, well-written sites. You're not in the answer.

"best med spa in [city]" "who does the best botox near me" "recommend a clinic for…"
Deficiency 03 · Design & conversion

Your site says budget, not premium.

An outdated design quietly tells visitors your clinic isn't worth a $3,000–$5,000 treatment — before they read a single word about your work.

Deficiency 04 · Response time

You're invisible after hours.

Most aesthetic research happens in the evening. Without an instant-response chatbot, the highest-intent visitors leave with their questions unanswered.

A typical scenario

It's 10:14pm. A patient who's been considering Botox for six months is on your treatments page, comparing you to two competitors. She has one question about pricing for her first appointment.

Your form says "we'll get back to you within one business day." She doesn't wait. She opens the next tab — your competitor has a chat widget that answers her in eight seconds and books her for Saturday.

You lost the patient at the exact moment her intent was highest.
