Why Hyperworkflow

Outcomes, not buzzwords.

Every agent and automation we build is wired around six outcomes — and we obsess over them.

Put the busywork on autopilot.

Custom automations erase the tedious, repetitive work — data entry, scheduling, follow-ups, reporting, document handling. Most teams reclaim 10–20+ hours a week to spend on actual growth.

Never let a lead go cold.

AI agents answer Instagram DMs and website inquiries in seconds, 24/7 — qualifying, answering, and booking before your competitor has even seen the message. Every lead stays warm.

Reply in seconds, not hours.

Businesses that respond to a lead within five minutes are ~21× more likely to convert it. Our agents reply instantly, every time — so speed stops being the reason you lose deals.

Built around the tools you already use.

Agents and automations plug into your CRM, inbox, calendar, Slack, spreadsheets, and website — and most tools with an API. We build around your stack. No rip-and-replace.

Humans stay in control.

Agents work inside clear guardrails and hand off to your team the moment a conversation needs a person. They defer instead of guessing — and you can review every action they take.

See exactly what's working.

A plain-English dashboard shows hours saved, leads captured, conversations handled, and what each automation is doing. Decisions from data, not gut feel.

What does Hyperworkflow actually build?

AI agents and automations, custom-built for your business. That includes agents that answer Instagram DMs, website chat, and email inquiries — qualifying leads and booking them around the clock — plus automations that remove repetitive manual work like data entry, scheduling, follow-ups, and reporting.

What kinds of processes can you automate?

If it's repetitive and follows rules or language patterns, it's usually automatable — lead replies, data entry, scheduling, follow-ups, reporting, document handling, and support triage are common ones. The free automation audit tells you which are worth it for your business.

Which industries do you work with?

All kinds — e-commerce, real estate, home services, healthcare and wellness, professional services, hospitality, agencies, and SaaS. The patterns we fix — slow lead response and manual busywork — repeat across nearly every industry.

How does the AI agent handle Instagram DMs and website inquiries?

It replies in seconds, answers questions, qualifies the lead, and books or routes them — 24/7. When something's outside its scope, it hands off to your team and captures the contact so no lead is lost.

Will the agent say something wrong or off-brand?

No. Agents are bounded to your business and guardrailed to its tone and policies. When unsure, they defer to a human rather than guess — and you can review every conversation.

How long until something is live?

Most first automations go live in about two weeks. Larger, multi-step builds take three to four weeks. We start with the single highest-impact win, then expand from there.

Do you integrate with the tools we already use?

Yes. Agents and automations plug into your CRM, inbox, calendar, Slack, spreadsheets, your website, and most tools with an API. We build around your stack — no rip-and-replace.

Do we own what you build?

Yes. You own the code, the integrations, the data, and the accounts. No platform lock-in — if you ever leave Hyperworkflow, everything stays with you.

How much does it cost?

Starter automations begin around $1,800 one-time. Most clients choose a custom build from $4,800. Ongoing automation-partner retainers start at $2,500/month. All pricing is on this page.
