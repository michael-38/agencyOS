Most clinic sites convert under 2% of visitors. A conversion-tuned rebuild typically lifts that 3–4× in the first 90 days. The fix is design and clarity — not more ad spend.

Book consultations, even at 11 p.m.

Most patients research aesthetic and wellness providers between 9 p.m. and midnight. Without a 24/7 assistant, every after-hours visitor goes to the clinic that picks up first in the morning.

Most clinic sites are invisible for the searches that matter — "botox near me," "physical therapy [city]." Local SEO, GBP rebuild, and treatment-page IA fixes that in a quarter.

Load in under a second.

53% of mobile visitors abandon a page that takes longer than 3 seconds. Speed is the cheapest conversion lift you'll ever buy — and the first thing a Google AI snippet checks before citing you.

How much does a Hyperworkflow site cost?

Sites start at $2,400 one-time plus $149/month. Most clinics choose the Rebuild tier at $4,800 + $249/month. All pricing is on this page — no quote calls required.

How long until my site is live?

Two weeks for a Refresh, three weeks for a Rebuild, four weeks for Scale. We've shipped Refresh sites in seven days when a client moves quickly on content.

Which industries do you work with?

We specialize in med spas, physical therapy clinics, massage studios, and mental-health therapy practices. Adjacent verticals like dermatology, chiropractic, and acupuncture are also a strong fit.

What does the AI chatbot actually do?

It answers visitor questions about services, pricing, hours, and treatments, then captures contact information and books consultations straight to your calendar. It's trained on your existing services, FAQ, and treatment pages.

Can I see the chatbot before I hire you?

Yes. Try the live demo on this page. It's the same engine your visitors will use, pre-loaded with Hyperworkflow content instead of yours.

Will the chatbot say something embarrassing?

No. The bot is bounded — it can only answer questions about your practice. When asked something outside its scope, it says it'll have someone follow up and captures the visitor's email.

Do I own the website?

Yes. You own the domain, code, content, and leads. No platform lock-in, no proprietary CMS. If you ever leave Hyperworkflow, you take everything with you.

What if I already have a website?

We rebuild it. The free audit is the first step — we show you exactly what's leaking conversions on your current site, then propose what we'd change.

Do you run paid ads or off-site SEO?

We build sites that convert paid traffic well and rank locally. We don't run paid ad campaigns or off-site link building — we partner with specialists for those.

What's the catch?

No catch. The build is one-time, the care plan is month-to-month, and you keep the site if you leave. We make money because clients stay — not because they can't leave.
