What does Hyperworkflow actually build?

AI agents and automations built for your business — agents that answer DMs, website chat, and email around the clock, plus automations that kill repetitive work like data entry, scheduling, and reporting.

What kinds of processes can you automate?

If it's repetitive — lead replies, data entry, scheduling, follow-ups, reporting, document handling — it's probably automatable. The free audit tells you what's worth it.

Which industries do you work with?

All kinds — e-commerce, real estate, home services, healthcare, professional services, hospitality, agencies, and SaaS. Slow lead response and busywork show up everywhere.

How does the AI agent handle Instagram DMs and website inquiries?

It replies in seconds, qualifies the lead, and books or routes them — 24/7. Anything outside its scope goes to your team, with the contact captured so no lead is lost.

Will the agent say something wrong or off-brand?

No. Agents are bounded to your business and tone, and defer to a human when unsure. You can review every conversation.

How long until something is live?

Most first automations go live in about two weeks; bigger builds take three to four. We start with your highest-impact win.

Do you integrate with the tools we already use?

Yes — your CRM, inbox, calendar, Slack, spreadsheets, website, and most tools with an API. We build around your stack.

Do we own what you build?

Yes. You own the code, integrations, data, and accounts. No lock-in.

How much does it cost?

Starter automations from $1,800, custom builds from $4,800, partner retainers from $2,500/month. All pricing is on this page.
