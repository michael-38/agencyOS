## Tier 1 — Highest Value Targets ($10K+ per job)

### 1. Personal Injury Attorneys
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Case** | $13,000–$31,000 in fees (contingency) |
| **Annual Solo Revenue** | $500K+ (58% of PI solos) |
| **Marketing Spend** | 8–20% of revenue ($2.5B industry-wide on ads) |
| **US Practitioners** | ~50,000 PI firms |
| **How They Advertise** | Google Ads (most competitive keywords in existence), TV, billboards, LSAs, social media |
| **Where to Find Them** | State bar directories (44 states publish searchable databases), Avvo (auto-creates profiles for every US licensed attorney), Martindale-Hubbell, Google Maps, FindLaw |
| **Why Target Them** | Highest marketing spend of any profession. Already accustomed to paying for leads. One case covers months of your retainer. |

### 2. Pool Installation Contractors
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Job** | $45,000–$100,000 |
| **Marketing Spend** | 7–12% of revenue |
| **How They Advertise** | Google Ads, Houzz, yard signs, vehicle wraps, local SEO |
| **Where to Find Them** | State contractor license databases, Houzz, Google Maps, APSP member directory |
| **Why Target Them** | Extremely high ticket. One job easily covers a full year of marketing retainer. Seasonal urgency drives ad spend. |

### 3. Kitchen/Bath Remodel Contractors
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Job** | $25,000–$50,000 (mid-range remodel) |
| **Marketing Spend** | 5–10% of revenue |
| **How They Advertise** | Houzz, Google Ads, home shows, Angi, local SEO, portfolio websites |
| **Where to Find Them** | State contractor license boards, Houzz (2.7M+ pros), NARI member directory, Google Maps |
| **Why Target Them** | High ticket, visual work (great for social media marketing). Most are small shops with weak digital presence. |

### 4. Cosmetic Surgeons / Plastic Surgeons
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Procedure** | $5,000–$20,000 per surgical procedure |
| **Annual Practice Revenue** | $1M–$3M+ |
| **Marketing Spend** | 5–12% of revenue ($5K–$20K/month) |
| **How They Advertise** | Instagram, Google Ads, RealSelf, before/after galleries, influencer partnerships |
| **Where to Find Them** | RealSelf, ASPS member directory, state medical board databases, Google Maps, Healthgrades |
| **Why Target Them** | Extremely high ticket. Very image-conscious industry — they value polished marketing. Already spending heavily on digital. |

### 5. Roofing Contractors
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Job** | $9,000–$13,000 (full replacement) |
| **Annual Solo/Small Firm Revenue** | $500K–$2M |
| **Marketing Spend** | 5–12% of revenue |
| **US Practitioners** | ~96,000 businesses |
| **How They Advertise** | Google LSAs, door knocking, yard signs, vehicle wraps, direct mail, storm chasing |
| **Where to Find Them** | State contractor license databases (many offer CSV downloads), Google Maps, Angi, HomeAdvisor, GAF/Owens Corning contractor locators |
| **Why Target Them** | Huge volume of small operators. Most have terrible websites. High urgency service = high ad spend willingness. 2 jobs cover a full year of retainer. |

### 6. Real Estate Agents
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Avg Revenue Per Transaction** | $10,000–$24,000 (commission, varies by market) |
| **Median Annual Income** | $55,000–$65,000 (top 10% earn $200K+) |
| **Marketing Spend** | 10–20% of gross commission income |
| **US Practitioners** | ~1.5 million active NAR members; ~3.5M total licensees |
| **How They Advertise** | Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook/Instagram ads, Google Ads, direct mail, yard signs, personal branding |
| **Where to Find Them** | ARELLO national database (4.38M records from 45 jurisdictions), state real estate commission databases, Zillow agent directory, Realtor.com, Google Maps |
| **Why Target Them** | Largest addressable market by headcount. Most agents are independent contractors who manage their own marketing. Easy to find through state licensing databases. 1 closed deal pays for months of retainer. |

## Quick-Start Playbook

### Week 1: Free Lead Sources
1. **Apollo.io** — Sign up for free tier (10K email lookups/month). Search by job title + location for any profession above.
2. **Outscraper** — Free tier extracts 500 Google Maps businesses/month. Search "[profession] + [city]" and export.
3. **NPPES Bulk Download** — Download the entire US healthcare provider database for free. Filter by specialty (dentists, chiropractors, etc.).
4. **Your state's contractor license board** — Many offer downloadable CSV files with every licensed contractor.

### Week 2: Scale Up
5. **State bar directory** — Search and export attorney data for your target geography.
6. **ARELLO / state real estate commission** — Pull real estate agent data.
7. **Google Maps scraping** — Use Outscraper or Apify to extract all [roofing contractors / HVAC companies / etc.] in your target metro areas.

### Week 3: Enrich & Outreach
8. **Cross-reference** leads with Apollo.io or UpLead to get verified emails and direct phone numbers.
9. **Prioritize** businesses with poor websites, few Google reviews, or no social media presence — they need you most.
10. **Monitor** new business filings via Secretary of State for fresh leads.
